
Wiring
=================================================

The preferred scheme to wire :underline:`axis` to a stepper/servo 
motor driver is the common anode method while common cathode still possible.

.. https://docs.typo3.org/m/typo3/docs-how-to-document/main/en-us/WritingReST/Reference/Graphics/Images.html


Configuring

.. list-table::
   :widths: 25 25 
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Sensor type
     - Limit pin trigger

   * - NPN NO
     - Falling edge

   * - NPN NC
     - Rising edge
   
   * - PNP NO
     - Rising edge
   
   * - PNP NC 
     - Falling edge
