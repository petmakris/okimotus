Stepper configuration#

Axis Controller can produce up to 75000 steps per second (75KHz step rate). The maximum output RPM depends on the desired microstepping (Steps/Rev). The maximum output linear speed depends additionally on the Unit/Rev setting.

E.g. for microstepping x8 we configure for 1600 Steps/Rev. This yields a maximum output RPM of 2812.5.

Additionally, in metric units, for a 5mm ballscrew/leadscrew pitch this translates to 14062.5 mm/sec maximum output speed.


steps/sec (max) 75000
mm/rev 5
steps/rev 200
